    Research & Development

    Continuous research and development of new designs and new fabrics as per the fashion trends is running under the guidance of experienced designers, executives and skilled embroidery workers. Fashion forecasts watched, monitored and worked upon to meet the requirement of fashion industry.

    Quality Control & Commitment

    Full thrust is given on quality control and all the production is thoroughly checked by experienced team before packing and making any shipment.
